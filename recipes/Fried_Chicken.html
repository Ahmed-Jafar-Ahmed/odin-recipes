Fried Chicken

Description

For a general fried chicken recipe look wash chicken, then dry and coat in flour and seasoning then coat in egg then flour and seasoning again. Finally dunk in hot oil until crispy golden. Once done ENJOY!!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Drumstick
  • flour
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Eggs
  • OOIL

Steps

  1. wash chicken
  2. Drench in flour and seasoning
  3. Drench in egg
  4. Complete step 2 again
  5. Deep FRY
  6. EAT IT UPPP
