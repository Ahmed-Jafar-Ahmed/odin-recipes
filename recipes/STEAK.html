Steak

Description

I LOOOOVE STEAK. So I decided to just develop my own way of making it. Here it is!!!!

Ingredients

  • Steak (cut of your choice)
  • Seasonings
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat Pan
  2. Heat butter on pan
  3. Place steak on the pan
  4. Flip Steak on the pan
  5. Take Steak off of pan and ENJOY
